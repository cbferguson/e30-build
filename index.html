The Build

The Car

This is a personal restoration project — a labor of love for a car that deserves to be driven again. We're documenting every step of the process for friends and family to follow along.

From the initial teardown through bodywork, mechanical rebuilding, and the final reassembly, this site captures it all — the highs, the headaches, and the progress.

Check back regularly for new photos as the build develops.
